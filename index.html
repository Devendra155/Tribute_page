About the Legend

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was a renowned scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the “Missile Man of India”, he played a pivotal role in India's space and defense programs. His efforts made India a nuclear power.

What makes him truly admirable is not just his scientific brilliance but his humble, inspirational, and patriotic nature. He always believed in youth and dedicated his life to empowering students and dreamers.

His books like “Wings of Fire” and “Ignited Minds” are treasures of motivation. His speeches, filled with energy and vision, continue to inspire generations.

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
– Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
